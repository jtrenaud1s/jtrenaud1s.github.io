Incursion Guide

Scout Systems you need 3-5 Pilots. It is pretty simple and straight forward and good place for beginning pilots to test their skills.

Vanguard Systems are a bit challenging for first timer FCs. You need 5-10 (10 for max pay out) pilots for this. Normally most fleets will have 11 pilots. 3 Logistics and 8 DPS (7 DPS for max payout)

Assault Systems are quite fun when you want a change of pace. You need 10 – 20 pilots it is recommended you have 5 Logistics, 10 Sniper Battleships, and 5 T3/Cruiser hulls.

Headquarter Systems is where the fun can be at and many pilots spend all their time there. It is recommended to 20 – 40 pilots. 10 Sniper Battleships, 10 Logistics, 20 DPS of varying ships.

The Kundalini Manifest is the mother of all sites found only in the Headquarter System. Finishing this site will end the incursion in the constellation. You need a lot of people for this one max pay out is 30 to 50 pilots. 10 – 15 Logistics and everything else is DPS. Warning you need an FC who knows what they are doing along with a Logistics Commander that also knows what to do. Now many fleets run with 80 People in it before it becomes an eligable fleet for pay out.
